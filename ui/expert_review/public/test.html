What we mean by «the elite»

By elite we generally mean individuals, groups or organizations that wield more influence and power than most other citizens or groups in society, such as in politics, the economy, the media, religion or the state.

Concrete examples are:

  • important political parties, political representatives, influential social movements, or international organizations (for example the United Nations, the European Union, or the International Monetary Fund, etc.)
  • large (multinational) companies, their bosses and owners, as well as workers organizations
  • journalists, major TV channels, and big news outlets
  • churches and other religious organizations
  • bureaucrats and state agencies

Examples of general vs specific criticism

To answer question 3, you need to assess whether the elite criticism singles out certain wrong-doings, points of disagreement etc. OR whether it is instead broadly generalizing, for example by asserting an overall lack of integrity, competence, piety, honesty etc. Criticisms of the first type are specific. Criticisms of the second type are general.

The following post is an example of a general elite criticism:

... the @Popular is not only a structurally corrupt party, tb is structurally authoritarian and leads us towards the biggest rights cut in democracy!

In contrast, the criticisms of the next two posts are specific: The first post specifically criticizes ‘the State's’ failure to appropriately regulate competition. This second post criticizes ‘the PD’ and ‘the left's’ immigration policy.

I join the fight of @ETFranchise against the unfair competition of the #grandedistribution which destroys our shops downtown with the complicity of the State! Millions of m2 of illegal sales area! This must stop. #EnTouteFranchise ...

HOW DOES THE PD TALK TO STILL RECEPTION AND IMMIGRATION? The left has opened the gates wide, making reception an unmanageable phenomenon. @GiorgioSilli explains to Piero Fassino the errors of the Democratic Party on immigration. ...

Examples of posts that are not elite criticism

The types of posts that lack any type of implicit or explicit elite criticism is long. Some concrete cases with examples are:

  • policy-related appeals or policy proposals (e.g. ‘My current initiative to facilitate business: Stop the excess of penalties for businesses. ...’)
  • self-praise (e.g. ‘With #EenHeleGoedeMorgen we work every day on a country that we want to pass on. #teamCDA ...’)
  • positive appeals to social groups (e.g. ‘The elderly are the victims of the pension policy. According to calculations by @EIBnl, it is precisely the young people who will benefit in the future. 50PLUS has introduced own-initiative law to prevent discounts on pensions! ...’
  • “service posts” and other trivia (e.g. ‘Careful on the roads tonight folks. Massive downpours in some areas. #drivesafe’)
